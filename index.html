Hi, I'm Daniel, and I'm a full-stack developer based in Chicago, Illinois with experience in various client-facing roles, including technology consulting and software implementation. Thanks for visiting!


Prior to beginning my professional life, I studied Information Systems and Operations Management at Indiana University's Kelley School of Business, where I also earned an M.S. in Information Systems in 2019. It was during this time that I was first exposed to software engineering in a formal capacity.


As a technically-minded person, understanding the nuts and bolts of how things work at the most fundamental level is something that I've always gravitated towards. During my time as a consultant, I worked closely with developers on tasks like writing technical documentation and translating client needs into actionable specs, which inspired an even greater curiousity and desire to learn. After a few years of self-learning and exploration, I decided to make it my career.


In my leisure time, I love to travel, explore the outdoors, and subject myself to the pain that is being a New York Jets fan.
